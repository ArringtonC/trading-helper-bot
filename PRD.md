# Options Trading Analytics Platform – Research‑Driven Product Requirements Document\n\n*Version: May\xa010\xa02025 (living document)*\n\n---\n\n## How to Use This Document\n\nThis Product Requirements Document (PRD) is the **single source of truth** for what we are building, why we are building it, and how we will measure success.\n\n* **Executives** can skim the *Executive Summary*, *Why This Project Is Important*, and *Success Metrics* to understand value and ROI.\n* **Design & Engineering** teams rely on the *Functional Requirements* and *Workflows* to scope, build, and test features.\n* **Marketing & Customer Success** use the *Personas*, *Value Realization Diagram*, and *Roadmap* to plan launches and campaigns.\n* **Everyone** should consult the *Living‑Document Process* to know how updates are made and where to propose changes.\n\n---\n\n## 1 Executive Summary\n\n**Problem.** Active options traders—especially semi‑professionals juggling several brokers—spend ≈\xa014.7\xa0hours per week on manual CSV reconciliation, lack real‑time risk insight, and face an 85\xa0% first‑year failure rate driven by education and sizing gaps.\n\n**Solution.** A broker‑agnostic desktop‑to‑cloud analytics platform that:\n\n1. **Automates multi‑broker data ingest** (IBKR & Schwab CSV today, API sync tomorrow).\n2. **Visualises strategy edge & risk** with intuitive payoff/greeks charts and live gauges.\n3. **Guides position sizing** via goal‑based wizards and dynamic throttle rules.\n4. **Educates in context** through embedded tutorials and an engaged community.\n\n**Market Opportunity.** Options analytics TAM hits **$1.48\xa0B in 2025**; our initial sweet‑spot is the $296\xa0M semi‑pro segment plus $584\xa0M advanced‑beginner retail funnel.\n\n**Competitive Edge.** We bridge the gap between low‑cost visualisers (OptionStrat) and pricey institutional suites by delivering institutional‑grade automation at 10\xa0% of the cost.\n\n**Go‑to‑Market.** Freemium → Pro ($29) → Premium ($79) tiers anchored to quantifiable time savings. Acquisition via educational content, Discord community, and broker partnerships.\n\n**Roadmap Snapshot.** Desktop MVP is live; six monthly SaaS releases add dashboards, analytics, risk engine, community, API sync, and mobile.\n\n---\n\n## 2 Market Research Highlights\n\n| Insight                                               | Evidence (2024‑25)       | Product Impact
